Principal Functions


Automate customer communications

Intiza allows you to create rules for the automatic sending of emails, text messages (SMS) or WhatsApp messages to customers, customizing the content of the messages with customer information and their account status. In this way, you will be able to free your internal teams from the time they spend notifying new invoices, sending account statements and making claims for delinquent debt.

Automate internal alarms

Intiza allows you to set internal alarms on situations that require collectors' attention (eg, unfulfilled payment promises or disputes not resolved within the defined deadlines), so that collectors do not need to review all open cases to identify those that require attention.

Prioritize clients to manage

Intiza automatically prioritizes the clients to be managed, using rules that are based on debt, default and the procedures that have been carried out with them. In this way, your company ensures that the collectors' time (the scarcest resource) is focused where it provides the greatest value.

Simplify the work of collectors

ntiza simplifies the work of 1) Choosing the client to manage, 2) Preparing for the management, 3) Carrying out the management, 4) Recording the results of the management. In this way, collectors can manage a greater number of customers per day.

Analyze collections in real time

Intiza has dashboards, reports and metrics that allow collection management information to be obtained in real time. In this way, it is easier to diagnose, identify problems and propose solutions.

Integrate with any ERP or accounting system

Intiza integrates with any ERP or accounting system in the market, to take from there the updated debt of clients. This integration can be done either through FTP/FTPS/SFTP or via API.
